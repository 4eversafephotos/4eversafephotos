Autobooks

Forever Auto Books feature is designed to make creating photo books quick and effortless. With Auto Books, we can easily transform our digital photo collection into beautifully designed, professionally curated photo books with just a few clicks. The feature utilizes advanced algorithms and intelligent layout generation to automatically arrange and place photos in an artistically pleasing manner.
We simply select the desired photos or albums, choose the Auto Books option, and Forever takes care of the rest. The platform intelligently analyzes the images, organizes them based on dates, locations, or themes, and generates a professionally designed photo book layout. We can then review and customize the layout by adding or removing photos, adjusting page order, and making personal touches like captions or text.
Auto Books offers a hassle-free solution for creating stunning photo books without the need for manual design work. It saves us valuable time while ensuring we still have control and flexibility over the final product. Whether it&;s for personal enjoyment or as a heartfelt gift, Forever Auto Books feature makes preserving and sharing cherished memories as easy as ever.

Design and Print Books

Forever Design and Print feature allows us to unleash our creativity by designing and printing personalized products using our stored digital content. We can choose from a variety of customizable templates for photo books, calendars, cards, and more. With instinctive design tools, they can easily add photos, text, backgrounds, and embellishments to create unique and visually appealing designs. Once the design is complete, we can conveniently order professional-grade prints of our creations, ensuring the highest quality output. Forever Design and Print feature empowers us to showcase our memories in beautiful, tangible formats, making it ideal for creating personalized gifts or preserving cherished moments in a tangible way.
