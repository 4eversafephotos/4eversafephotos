What is FOREVER?

FOREVER is a digital platform designed to help us preserve and share our memories for generations to come. It offers a range of services to securely store and organize photos, videos, documents, and other personal content in a private and easily accessible manner. FOREVER provides us with the tools to curate our digital assets, create customized albums and collections, and even collaborate with others on shared projects.

One of the key features of FOREVER is its emphasis on long-term preservation. The platform employs industry-leading archival practices to ensure that the stored content remains safe and accessible over time. This includes redundant storage, multiple data centers, and rigorous data integrity checks. By utilizing FOREVER, we can have peace of mind knowing that our precious memories are protected against loss, damage, or outdatedness.
In addition to preservation, FOREVER also enables us to share our memories with friends, family, and future generations. It offers options to create and order high-quality photo books, calendars, and personalized gifts, making it easy to turn digital memories into tangible keepsakes. The platform also allows us to invite loved ones to view and contribute to shared albums, fostering collaboration and connection through the shared experience of memories.
Overall, FOREVER is a comprehensive digital preservation and sharing platform that empowers us to safeguard and celebrate our memories in a secure and meaningful way, ensuring that those memories will endure for our lifetime and beyond. Learn more here.

Security

At FOREVER.com, the security and privacy of our customers; memories and personal data are of utmost importance to us. We understand the sentimental value and irreplaceable nature of the memories entrusted to us, and we are committed to ensuring their protection through robust security measures.

Data Encryption: We employ state-of-the-art encryption technologies to safeguard your memoirs and personal information. All data stored on our servers is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms, ensuring that only authorized individuals can access it. Secure Infrastructure: Our infrastructure is designed with security in mind, utilizing modern security protocols and best practices. We regularly update and maintain our systems to address any vulnerabilities and stay ahead of emerging threats.

Access Controls: We have implemented strict access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data. Our employees undergo comprehensive background checks and receive security training to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of your memoirs.

Backup and Redundancy: To protect against data loss, we maintain multiple backups of your memories in geographically diverse locations. This redundancy ensures that your memoirs are preserved even in the unlikely event of a system failure or natural disaster.

Continuous Monitoring: We employ a dedicated team of security professionals who actively monitor our systems for any suspicious activity or potential breaches. In the event of any security incidents, we have response plans in place to mitigate and address them promptly.

Third-Party Audits: We regularly engage independent third-party auditors to assess the security of our systems and validate our practices. These audits help us identify any areas for improvement and ensure that we meet or exceed industry standards.

Transparent Privacy Policy: Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We are committed to transparency and will never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

User Control: We provide you with granular control over your memoirs, allowing you to choose the privacy settings and sharing options that best suit your preferences. You have the freedom to determine who can access and view your memories. While we strive to maintain the highest level of security, it's important to note that no system is completely immune to all risks. We encourage our users to follow good security practices, such as using strong and unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication. At FOREVER.com, we are dedicated to safeguarding your cherished memories and ensuring that they remain secure for generations to come.

Guarantee

We guarantee that the memories you store with FOREVER® will be preserved, protected, and available for your lifetime plus 100 years, with a goal of keeping your content safe and available as you have determined for many generations beyond. We provide ground-breaking rights and protections for your data, securing your FOREVER Storage® through good times and bad. Let's get technical. When you buy FOREVER® Storage, a large portion of your payment is deposited into the FOREVER® Guarantee Fund. The money is invested so that it increases in value and pays for the recurring maintenance and preservation costs of your FOREVER® Storage, as well as the migration of your content to new digital formats, over time. The FOREVER® Guarantee pays for the recurring maintenance and preservation costs of your Guaranteed Storage account. Similar to a university endowment, the money invested in the FOREVER® Guarantee Fund is carefully managed to fund the preservation of your content over a very long period of time. The money in the FOREVER® Guarantee Fund is fully restricted, meaning that we can only withdraw a very small amount from the Fund each year and the money withdrawn must be used to preserve and protect your precious digital content. Life insurance companies use carefully managed reserve funds to make sure they can always pay claims. Universities use carefully managed endowments to pay for their operations over hundreds of years. In the same way, FOREVER® will use the FOREVER® Guarantee Fund to save, protect, and make your information available for generations into the future.
