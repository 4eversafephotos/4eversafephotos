Many of the photo sharing platforms out there have an interesting condition in their terms of service. At any time, they can delete your photos. Yep, any time. Google Photos, Shutterfly, Facebook, and more. In fact, Google Photos will delete you pictures and memories if it learns you're dead. Not with Forever.

Forever can digitize your old photos, your VHS tapes, anything that is going out of date. They return your originals to you, along with a digitized version to help you keep up with the world. Not only that, but you don't have to fear damage from natural disasters ruining your only copy.

Forever makes it easy to pass along your digital copies, in fact, when you create an account, you get the storage for your lifetime, plus 100 years. That way your memories can be safe for generations onward.
